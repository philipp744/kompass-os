# Identifier Rules

Kompass OS uses stable identifiers for relevant objects.

Format:

- M-001 market
- ZG-001 target group
- PRO-001 problem
- BC-001 business case
- PF-001 product family
- PRD-001 product
- LP-001 landing page
- DEC-001 decision

Rules:

1. Identifiers are stable.
2. Names may change, identifiers do not.
3. Rejected ideas keep their identifiers.
4. Relationships should reference identifiers, not only names.

Initial pilot identifiers:

- M-001 Camping and motorhome
- BC-001 Camping organization systems
- PF-001 Cable management
- PF-002 Document organization
- PF-003 Storage organizers
