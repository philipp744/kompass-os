# BC-001 Decision Map

## Purpose
A readable map of the current business decisions for BC-001.

## Current thesis
Camping organisation is attractive because space is limited, recurring use creates repeated friction and several related problems can be solved as a system.

## Strongest path
M-001 -> BC-001 -> PRO-002 -> PF-001 -> PRD-008 -> LP-001

Meaning:
- Market: Camping and motorhome
- Business Case: organisation systems
- Problem: no fixed place for charging accessories
- Product family: cable management
- Product: cable station
- Landingpage: LP-001 Kabelstation

## Current decisions

| ID | Decision | Reason | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| DEC-BC001-001 | Start with cable station | clear problem and concrete use case | active |
| DEC-BC001-002 | Do not build shop first | validation before infrastructure | active |
| DEC-BC001-003 | Use landingpage before supplier commitment | demand first, sourcing second | active |
| DEC-BC001-004 | Treat storage products carefully | risk of being generic | active |
| DEC-BC001-005 | Keep GUI-readiness in data model | later dashboard should be easy | active |

## Next decision gates

### Gate 1: Landingpage readiness
Can LP-001 collect meaningful interest signals?

Required:
- live page
- form storage
- analytics
- privacy/imprint if public

### Gate 2: Demand signal
Does the audience react to the problem and product promise?

Required:
- enough traffic
- CTA clicks
- email opt-ins
- qualitative feedback

### Gate 3: Product feasibility
Can the idea become a realistic product?

Required:
- supplier research
- rough cost model
- realistic product variants
- mounting or placement concept

### Gate 4: Business case upgrade
Does LP-001 justify moving BC-001 forward?

Possible outcomes:
- develop product
- content-first route
- reposition
- pause
- reject

## Readable status
BC-001 is not yet a proven business case. It is a structured opportunity with a first validation prototype.
