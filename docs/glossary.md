# Glossary

## BC
Business Case. A structured opportunity consisting of market, target group, problem, solution direction and validation path.

## LP
Landingpage. A page used to validate demand before building a full shop.

## PRD
Product. A concrete product idea or product concept.

## PF
Product Family. A group of related products that solve connected problems.

## PRO
Problem. A relevant customer pain point.

## ZG
Target Group. A clearly described customer or user segment.

## SIT
Situation. A use context where a problem appears.

## HYP
Hypothesis. An assumption that still needs validation.

## DEC
Decision. A documented business or architecture decision.

## EXP
Experiment. A validation activity such as a landingpage test.

## Knowledge Graph
A structured model connecting markets, problems, target groups, products and experiments.

## Decision Engine
The scoring and recommendation logic used to decide what happens next.

## Validation
Testing whether a real audience reacts to a hypothesis.

## Prototype
A technical or visual test asset. It is not automatically a product or shop.

## Source of Truth
The most authoritative place for a specific type of information.
