# Decision Engine v1

Decision = Evidence, not opinion.

Inputs:
- Market
- Target group
- Problem intensity
- Search intent
- Competition
- Supplier feasibility
- Validation data

Outputs:
- Opportunity score
- Risk score
- Confidence score
- Recommended next action

Allowed actions:
- Research
- Landingpage
- Prototype
- Supplier search
- SEO
- Pause
- Reject
- Shopify readiness

Every recommendation must reference supporting entities.
