# LP-002 Doc Station

## Goal

Validate demand for a fixed document and checklist station in a camper.

## Hypothesis

Camper users need one fixed place for vehicle papers, campsite notes, checklists and travel documents.

## Target groups

- ZG-003 Motorhome couples
- ZG-005 Vanlife beginners
- ZG-001 Van owners

## Problems

- PRO-003 Documents and checklists are spread across the vehicle
- PRO-007 Single products do not create a full system

## Product

- PRD-009 Dokumentenstation

## Value proposition

All important travel and vehicle documents in one dedicated place.

## Page structure

1. Hero: One place for all camper documents
2. Problem: papers and checklists get lost inside the vehicle
3. Solution: a dedicated document station
4. Use cases: departure, campsite, control, travel planning
5. Product idea: folder plus station plus checklist system
6. CTA: join launch list
7. Lead magnet: camper document checklist

## CTA

Primary: join launch list

Secondary: get camper document checklist

## Success signal

Continue if the checklist performs better than a generic product CTA. That would support a content-first approach.
